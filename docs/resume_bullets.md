# Resume Bullet Points — AI Agent Workflow Observability Project

---

**Data Engineering**  
Engineered a reproducible synthetic dataset of 400 enterprise support tickets in Python using NumPy and Pandas, modeling realistic cross-variable correlations across 22 fields including confidence-band-dependent escalation probabilities, hallucination flags, SLA breach logic, and tiered cost structures — simulating a production AI support workflow at enterprise scale.

**KPI Analysis**  
Designed and computed a 20-metric KPI framework spanning AI reliability, workflow efficiency, customer satisfaction, and cost attribution, segmented by issue category, priority level, and confidence band; exported five Tableau-ready analytical datasets identifying a 3.5x cost differential between AI-handled and escalated tickets.

**Data Visualization**  
Built a 10-chart visualization suite in Matplotlib including a workflow friction heatmap, confidence score distribution with threshold annotations, escalation funnel, and scatter analysis of AI confidence vs. customer rating, surfacing that hallucinations reduced average customer ratings by 0.9 points and increased repeat-contact probability by 10 percentage points.

**Business Recommendations**  
Translated quantitative findings into 10 operational recommendations across routing automation, human governance, knowledge base development, and KPI monitoring — including a category-specific confidence threshold proposal estimated to reduce unnecessary escalations by 15 percentage points and save over $2,000 per 400-ticket window.

**End-to-End Analytics Project**  
Independently scoped and delivered a full-stack analytics project — from data generation and cleaning through KPI computation, visualization, and executive communication — demonstrating the ability to own an analytics workstream from raw data to leadership-ready insights for an enterprise AI operations use case.
